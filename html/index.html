Lab Grown Diamonds Are
Real Diamonds

Lab grown diamonds are classified Type IIa – the rarest and purest form of diamonds, never mined and guaranteed conflict-free. By recreating the natural diamond growing process in the laboratory, we are able to grow diamonds – without the social and environmental impacts associated with mined diamonds.

Every lab grown diamond has its own unique growth patterns and inclusions. Not one is the same as the other. Get your very own diamond without the high price tag and unethical concerns.

Lab grown diamonds are chemically, physically and optically identical to mined diamonds. The only difference is their origin.

Sam & J diamonds are certified Ideal Cut, D – E Colour and Flawless (FL) – VS1 Clarity grades with no fluorescence by independent gemological institutes such as GIA and IGI. We also provide magnified HD 360° videos of all our diamonds, so you get to know exactly what you are getting.

In addition, we work very closely with our diamond cutters. Through our stringent quality control, only the best diamonds are available for your selection.
